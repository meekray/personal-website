180 Degrees Consulting

I am currently a Project Manager leading a team of 4 Analysts to consult for an international environmental NGO.


180 Degrees Consulting is the world's largest university-based consultancy. We consult for local and international social-enterprises in order to help them maximize their social impact.


Before becoming a PM, I worked as an Analyst for two engagements. In my first engagement, I consulted for an international micro-finance nonprofit, helping them scale their impact through the implementation of a new technology product. Our team advised them on defining the features for the product and structuring its Go-to-market strategy.

In my second engagement as an Analyst, I consulted for a local Detroit startup, helping structure its product prioritization and define a marketing strategy in order to maximize its national reach.

Peerspace

In Summer 2017, I was an Expansion Intern in Peerspace's NYC Office.


Through its online marketplace, Peerspace is disrupting the way people think about accessing commercial real estate. This was my first exposure to the innovation that takes place in Silicon Valley, and was ultimately what sparked my interest to work in technology.


As an intern, I took on an exciting project - I researched how Guests and Hosts behave on the marketplace. The short-term objective of this project was to quantify the impact specific listing attributes (amenities, location, host history, etc.) had on a Peerspace host's value perception of his/her own property. The long term objective was for the data analyzed and conclusion drawn from this project to be used as the kickstart for a future price-recommending product feature, similar to Zillow's Zestimate.

I gained first hand experience in advanced Excel-modeling, A/B Testing, user-research, client-acquisition, and project-management.
